About Cardinalspeed

What this is

This site is a few things. It's a project that I decided to put some time into to learn more about coding tools. Did you know that it's possible to host a basic HTML page on github with a free account? I didn't know that. And now that I do, I wanted a place to play around with code and design and just see what it could be. There isn't any agenda here other than learn. I may post some personat things, and I may post some things that only I care about. I want to let the pages develop as they decide to develop.

Who writes it

Who me? I'm Matt and I have a life-long love of wheeled machines. That is my old BMW 2002 featured on some of these pages. I have a ton of history in the bicycle industry. I even built custom steel bicycles under the name Signal Cycles for a number of years. Now I design experiences for a small startup here in Austin, Texas.

Why the name

Back to wheels and movement. It's who I am and what I like to do, though I'm not an adrenaline junkie, I still get a thrill from feeling the wind on my face.
